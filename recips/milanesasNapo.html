Milanesa Napolitana's Recipes

Description

Milanesa Napolitana it's a famous plate between the Argentinian comunity. It's basachely compous of cow meat or chicken breast covering with eggs, previusly mixed with salt garlic and pepper. covered with bread crumbs and baked with tomato souce and muzzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • cow meat / chicken breast
  • eggs
  • tomato souce
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • parsley
  • muzzarella cheese

Steps

  1. breack the eggs and mixed it with garlic, pepper and salt
  2. put the meat in the mix and reserve for 15 mins in the fridge
  3. covert it all with bread crumbs
  4. cook in the oven at 190 degrees for 25 mins
  5. cover it with tomato souce, add the cheese and cook for another 10 mins
  6. Ready to serve!
