Pollo al horno's RECIPES

Description

Pollo al horno it's a famous plate between the Argentinian comunity. It's basachely compous of cow meat or chicken breast covering with eggs, previusly mixed with salt garlic and pepper. covered with bread crumbs and baked with tomato souce and muzzarella cheese

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Potato
  • onion
  • garlic
  • pepper
  • parsley
  • paprika

Steps

  1. put the chicken in a oven plate
  2. cut the potato and garlic and put in the plate
  3. spicy it as younlike
  4. cook in the oven at 190 degrees for 25 mins
  5. Ready to serve!
